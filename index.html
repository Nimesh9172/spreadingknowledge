About Spreading Knowledge

spreading knowledge is subsidiary of Pauras art vision which was established in 1989 as a production house and one roof training center for media courses.

Our vision

Enhance and enable personal skills. Empower students with creative and entrepreneurial skills. Develop the complete personality of the student through quality education and skill development activities. To reach and spread education at the grassroots level by using the online platform. To create a transformative educational experience for students with a focus on deep disciplinary knowledge; problem-solving; leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills; and personal health and well-being. Broaden educational Horizons.
