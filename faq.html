Frequently Asked Questions

I did not learn German or any other foreign language in the past. Can I still learn German ?

Yes. You are not expected to know German or any other foreign language before signing up for this German language course.

I have learnt a foreign language before. Will that interfere with German ?

Yes. You are not expected to know German or any other foreign language before signing up for this German language course.

I am not from English medium school. Can I still learn German ?

Yes. It is a myth that only students from English medium school can learn German. Students from all other mediums can learn equally well.

I did not learn German or any other foreign language in the past. Can I still learn German ?

Yes. You are not expected to know German or any other foreign language before signing up for this German language course.

Do you start from basics ?

Yes.

Is German easy ?

Yes. This is a basic course. It is more practical and application oriented and participants get training in all language skills i.e. listening, reading, speaking, etc. .
