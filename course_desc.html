German A1.1 Course

Course Syllabus

Methodology

Instruction, question-answer, discussion, collaboration in pairs and groups

Learning objectives of the course:

1. To enhance listening and oral skills

2. Step by step tests on the following themes: Greetings and introductions, meeting new colleagues, in hotel, initiating small talk, talking about travelling

3. To widen and deepen vocabulary, grammar and expressions through various types of exercises and tests

Learning outcomes of the course:

1. Can understand and use familiar everyday expressions and basic phrases aimed at the satisfaction of needs of a concrete type.

2. Can introduce themselves and others and can ask and answer questions about personal details such as where they live, people they know and things they have.

3. Can interact in a simple way provided the other person talks slowly and clearly and is prepared to help.
